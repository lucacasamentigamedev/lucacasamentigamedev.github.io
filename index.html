Beat Of Rebellion

Beat Of Rebellion

Type

3D, Hack & Slash, Action, Rhythm Game, Isometric

Game info

  • Period: 2023 - today
  • Engine: Unreal Engine
  • Status: In progress

Description

A young musician lives in a dystopian cyberpunk city where all artistic disciplines such as music, theater, dance, literary arts, media arts, and visual arts are forbidden, because they distract humans from working efficiency and prevent them from being productive, disciplined and strict. In such a society that values only the common good in a twisted way, artists are considered a system mistake and branded as criminals; the young musician has to fight the authorities to affirm his freedom and to spread the importance of music and emotions to all of society.

What I've Done in the Game

  • Developed many Main Character systems and mechanics like:
    • Dash (on floor and in air, on beat) with camera FOV manipulation
    • Animation Blueprint with different animation preset (dash & combat) based on zone maps
    • Player Behind Wall highlight
    • Circular buffer of 4 notes under player that every attack collect a note (green or red base on beat input) and when full cast a more powerful attack or a melody, if unlocked
  • As regards the musical component i've realized:
    • FMOD integration with Unreal plugin creating an Audio Manager blueprint for play one shot or background sound, this lasts can blend from explore to combat and reverse with in-game event or map triggers. With the help of plugin i've extracted the beat event to permit to every actor or system to pulse or to do something on beat
    • The Metronome blueprint for the core mechanic to determine if the player is in sync with the beat or note
    • A Simon Says mini-game where the player has to repeat the sequenceof notes played by the game on beat
  • Combat camera in fight that adapts fov based on the proximity to enemies
  • A system that counts the time it takes the player to complete an arena, stopping when there are dialogues and when the game is paused
  • A Debug Log system to print log based on category, colors, time on screen, ecc
  • Contributed to numerous other developments and minor fixes or features including UI, widgets, design or whatever the project needed

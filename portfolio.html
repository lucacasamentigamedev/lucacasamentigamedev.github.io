Beat Of Rebellion

Hack & Slash, Action, Rhythm Game, Isometric



Description


A young musician lives in a dystopian cyberpunk city where all artistic disciplines such as music, theater, dance, literary arts, media arts, and visual arts are forbidden, because they distract humans from working efficiency and prevent them from being productive, disciplined and strict. In such a society that values only the common good in a twisted way, artists are considered a system mistake and branded as criminals; the young musician has to fight the authorities to affirm his freedom and to spread the importance of music and emotions to all of society.


What I've Done in the Game


  • Developed the main character, including combat system, animations, Animation Blueprint, dash, and interactions. Interactions around the player occur through base interaction or through the buffer of played notes.
  • Set up, managed, and integrated the entire audio system of the game using FMOD and the related Unreal plugin.
  • Created the metronome system and managed the current background song beat to determine if the player is in sync with the beat and act accordingly.
  • Contributed to numerous other developments and minor fixes including UI, widgets, design, debug log systems, and any other project needs that arose.

Fish & Kiss (Global Game Jam 2024)

Simulation, Point & Click, 2.5D



Jam info


  • Jam: Global Game Jam 2024, AIV - Accademia Italiana Videogiochi cluster
  • Year: 2024
  • Event Dates: 22/01/2024, 15:00 - 28/01/2024, 23:00
  • Languages: English
  • Theme: Make Me Laugh
  • Game: https://globalgamejam.org/games/2024/fish-kiss-6
  • Repo: https://github.com/lucacasamentigamedev/Fish-And-Kiss

Description


"Fish & Kiss" is a simulation, point & click game where you matchmake for fish using a dating app called Finder. You browse fish profiles, pick one, and catch it. Then, you decorate a room for your dinner date with furniture that matches the fish's tastes. During the date, you choose the right dialogue options. If it goes well, the fish becomes a trophy in your aquarium.


What I've Done in the Game


  • Partecipate to Game design and concept development
  • Integration of musical elements, design and implementation of widgets, and general development tasks and problem-solving
